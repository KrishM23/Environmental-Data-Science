Where to go next at UCLA

UCLA degrees, capstones, fellowships, and research programs in one place. Each entry covers who it is for, what you will do, when to apply, and links to the official page.

Step 1: Explore

Still building skills?

Start with starter projects and datasets, then look at coursework like ENVIRON 175 or the ESS minor to deepen your toolkit.

Step 2: Apply

Ready for real clients?

Senior Practicum, NASA DEVELOP and the Luskin EJ Fellowship put you on teams solving live environmental problems, often with stipends.

Step 3: Commit

Thinking long-term?

Compare IoES degrees (B.S., Ph.D., D.Env.), the Leaders in Sustainability certificate and campus initiatives like Sustainable LA or DataX.
